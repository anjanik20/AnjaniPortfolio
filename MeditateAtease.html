Meditate At Ease

Meditation Mobile-Application

UX CASE STUDY

Project: Designed a meditation mobile-application that offers variety of meditation sessions guiding users on a journey towards calmness and tranquility.

Project Type: Individual

Duration: 4 months

Tools used: Figma, Photoshop

Objective

The objective of designing the mobile-application Meditate At Ease is to provide a simple and intuitive platform that enables users to meditate anytime anywhere easily. The application provides a diverse range of meditation techniques with varying experience levels - beginner, intermediate and advanced. Users can set reminders as well to maintain consistency in their mindfulness journey. Meditate At Ease aims to ensure an accessible and seamless user-experience is provided, promoting a peaceful and balanced state of mind for all.

Problem Statement

In today’s fast pace world, many people are finding ways to manage stress, anxiety and improve their mental well-being however they face challenges in creating and maintaining a consistent meditation practice. Multiple individuals struggle finding time in busy schedules, receiving guidance which aligns with their personal goals and staying motivated. Without a structured and effective system users find it challenging to stay with meditation practice and find it hard to inculcate mindfulness in their lives. In need of a meditation app that offers multiple meditation practices along with progress tracking as well as set reminders. It will help users to be consistent and promote mindfulness and reduce stress.

Project Goals

  • Tailor meditation exercises based on user’s proficiency level making it personalized.
  • Provide a variety of meditation practices. Additionally, include insightful podcasts which would help users to understand the importance of meditation and benefit users.
  • Incorporate reminder notifications to encourage users to create a consistent routine in their mindfulness journey.
  • Provide user-friendly experience so that users can navigate from one section to another easily.

Research

The journey of designing meditation app started off by understanding the needs and preferences of users. To begin with, in person interviews were conducted as well as google form was distributed. Through this process users’ interest in meditation practices, their experiences with stress, anxiety, sadness was recognized and their familiarity with existing meditation apps was explored. This research provided valuable insights into the features the users desired, challenges they faced as well as their overall perception towards digital solutions for mindfulness was known. Their pain points, frustrations, dislikes were acknowledged. Building upon these insights, user personas were created which included user goals, motivation, preferences and challenges. The personas acted as a foundational guide throughout the design process, ensuring that the meditation app was effective and designed in a such manner which would offer a seamless experience that aligns with users’ needs and enhances their meditation practice.

  • 6. What features of the meditation app you're using now or have used before do you think are the most helpful?
    • For the people who have not used a meditation application before
  • 7. What has prevented you from using a meditation app?
  • 8. What would encourage you to try a meditation app for the first time?
    • Additional Questions Asked

Personas

Meera Raut, a 19 year old architecture student who recently relocated to a new city for college is struggling with stress, anxiety, pressure while adapting to college life. She needs a beginner friendly meditation app that offers wide range of meditation techniques she can access anytime, includes progress tracker and is easy to navigate. Her goals are to improve mental health, manage exam pressure and be able to have a consistent meditation routine.

Araav Desai, is a 30 year old software engineer from Bangalore who experiences high level of stress and pressure because of his demanding job and finds difficulty in balance his work life and emotional well-being. He seeks for a user-friendly meditation app that offers meditation sessions based on proficiency along with reminder notifications to help build a consistent routine. Araav’s goal is to improve productivity, reduce stress and pressure and be able to balance work life and personal life.

Transformative Meditation with AR/VR

An idea for incorporating AR/VR into Meditate At Ease--Meditation app

A visualize button for each meditation technique/session can be provided. By clicking this visualize button and using a VR headset, users can be transported into relaxing and pleasant environments which will allow users to disconnect from the surroundings and engage more deeply with the meditation techniques given. This immersive meditation practice will enable users to connect with their inner selves and be in a calm state of mind.

Accessibility

Accessibility is important and to ensure an inclusive experience ,following ideas are considered for meditation application.

  • Provided text-to-speech option for each meditation technique.
  • For each technique pictures have been provided offering visual guidance.
  • Clear labels provided.
  • Made sure there is sufficient color contrast between the text and the background for readability.

Accessibility ideas that can be incorporated into Meditate At Ease(Meditation app)

  • The meditation reminders can include audible alert to make it easier to understand when reminder has popped up.
  • At the end of each meditation technique a vibration signal can be added to indicate completion of meditation session which would really help users with hearing impairments.

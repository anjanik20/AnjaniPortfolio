ShieldHer

(Self Defense Mobile-Application For Women)

UX CASE STUDY

Project: Designed a Self-Defense mobile-application for women to empower and boost their confidence by providing comprehensive resources and techniques to enhance their personal safety.

Project Type: Individual

Duration: 4 months

Tools used: Figma, Photoshop

Objective

The objective of ShieldHer—Self-Defense mobile application is designed specifically for women. The mobile-application aims to empower women and make them confident to protect themselves in dangerous situations. The application provides access to wide range of self-defense techniques. Moreover, self-defense techniques are also provided for senior women and women with sensory disabilities. To enhance safety ShieldHer includes a SOS button and Emergency contact button to ensure that users can easily seek help in emergencies. Users can track their progress, set goals and also stay informed through relevant blogs and articles related to self-defense and personal safety through the application. ShieldHer aims to deliver user-friendly experience and prioritizes accessibility.

Problem Statement

Many women face increased concerns about personal safety and they are in need of effective self-defense strategies. Despite the rising acknowledgment of the challenges faced, there is still a significant lack of easily accessible resources and user friendly tools to provide vulnerable individuals with essential self-defense skills and knowledge. Moreover, users many a times struggle to find appropriate self-defense resources which could lead to frustration and lack of motivation. In need of a self-defense app that offers comprehensive tutorials, progress tracking dashboard, podcasts and articles designed for all women users. Ultimately enabling users to boost confidence, make learning easy and provide a sense of community as a whole which will enable users to feel safe and empowered in their everyday lives.

Project Goals

  • Offer self defense tutorials labelled as beginner, intermediate and advanced ensuring users receive appropriate instruction based on their current abilities. Additionally, self-defense techniques for senior women and woman with sensory disabilities are also provided.
  • A Progress tracking dashboard that allows users to monitor their progress. Moreover, users can write personalized goals and set reminders to it.
  • Allow users to store emergency contact information and provide SOS button ensuring immediate assistance is provided.
  • Provide users with articles and blogs related to self-defense practices.
  • Offer an easy navigation and smooth user-friendly interface to enhance accessibility and engagement.

Research

The design of ShieldHer(Self-defense app) began by gaining a clear understanding of safety concerns and challenges faced by woman. To gather insights both google form survey was distributed and in person interviews were conducted This research allowed to get a deeper understanding of women’s perception of personal safety, their familiarity with self defense techniques and the challenges they face. Additionally, users comfort with digital tools and whether they have used any self-defense apps or resources was known. The research helped uncover users’ paint points and frustrations. Users’ motivations and needs were recognized. Moreover, users shared what features they would like to see and what things were missing. Building on this research, user personas were created highlighting users goals, motivation, challenges and preferences. The personas played a really crucial role in guiding the design process, making sure that the end result would be a user-centred solution that effectively addresses the needs of all users.

Questionnaire

  • 1. How confident do you feel while using digital products?
  • 2. How prepared do you feel currently to protect yourself in dangerous situations
  • 3. Have you ever considered using a self-defense application before?
  • 4. How do you currently learn or seek information about self-defense?
  • 5. What types of self-defense training or resources are you most interested in?
  • 6. What features are crucial to you in a self-defense application if designed one?
  • 7. Are there any concerns or barriers that you have faced if used a self defense application in the past or currently, if using a self-defense application?
  • 8. What additional features or content would make a self-defense app more appealing to you?
  • 9. What kind of accessibility features would you expect from a self-defense app?

Personas

Ankita is a 38 year old high school teacher. She is a single mother and it’s important for her to balance work life and family life. She has very busy schedules. She is uncertain about her safety outside the walls of her school therefore she is highly motivated to take action for her own security and her family. She needs a self defense application that offers variety of self defense techniques and also includes beginner friendly sessions. Additionally, she would also like articles, blogs related to staying safe and protecting oneself to be included in the self defense mobile application . Her goal is to feel safe and feel confident, continue learning new self defense techniques and motivate others to take charge of their personal safety.

Mrs. Pratibha is a 65 year old widow who lives in Pune with her son. She focuses on maintaining her independence and stays active by going on morning walks, she goes for meditation practices as well as visits the local market. When travelling alone she is often anxious, particularly while using the public transport or walking on the streets alone. She is in need of a self defense application which would provide easy access to an SOS button and emergency contact button which would ensure immediate help in case of unsafe situations. She wants a dependable platform where she would be able to set reminders and track progress to ensure consistency. Her goal is to increase confidence and feel more secure.

Additional Feature Added

Additionally, to keep the users motivated an achievement system idea introduced. Each time when on completing a self-defense technique within a specific category, users will earn a star. Upon collecting two stars users will earn a badge. The more the badges earned within the specific category the more the progress and techniques learned will be reflected. This system aims to encourage users, boost their confidence and motivate them as they master each technique step by step.

Augmented Reality:Empowering and Enhancing Self-Defense Techniques for Women

An idea for incorporating AR into ShieldHer--Self Defense Mobile-Application for Women.

Augmented Reality(AR) can be used to provide real-time feedback to check whether user’s posture while practicing specific self-defense techniques is correct or incorrect. A feedback button can be added to the mobile-application for each self-defense technique. Upon pressing the feedback button the application will ask for the permission of the user to access the camera. Once permission granted, an overlay will be activated to assess users posture. Green color will indicate the posture of the user is correct while the red color will provide areas where the posture is incorrect. Additionally, voice feedback can be incorporated through which verbal comments can be given to inform the users where they are going wrong.

Accessibility

Accessibility is crucial ensures that all users can fully engage and benefit from the application.These are some accessibility considerations taken into account for ShieldHer( Self Defense Application for Women).

  • Provided text-to-speech option for each self defense technique for women.
  • Information and instructions provided before starting the application.
  • Visual representations provided for each self defense technique
  • Clear labels provided.

Takeaway

ShieldHer- Self-defense application for woman provides a platform for women who are seeking empowerment and safety. Designed an application where multiple self-defense techniques offered to woman which also includes self-defense techniques for senior and those with disabilities. Making sure that everyone can benefit. The application features an emergency call button and an SOS button which is present on the start screen as well as in the navigation bar. The self-defense techniques are also labelled for beginners, Intermediate and advanced so that women can choose according to their skill set. Personal goals can be written down by users and reminders can be set accordingly. To enhance motivation an achievement system is introduced in the application. Accessibility was a crucial component throughout the design process. I thoroughly enjoyed designing this application from research phase to high fidelity design. The feedback received throughout the design process was really valuable for creating a user friendly solution. I am eager and excited to keep improving the application based on new ideas and user-insights which includes expanding the range of self-defense techniques for women with disabilities. Making sure that the content provided is relevant to all users. I am really enthusiastic to incorporate the idea AR into the application in the future.
